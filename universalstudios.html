Summary

I went to Universal Studios on June 11 with some of my family and friends. My friend's family went in a different car. It was supposed to take our family forty minutes to get from our hotel to Universal Studios. Instead, it took two hours. That's because we made a stop at a Vietnamese bakery, went the wrong way, and got stuck in traffic. All of us stayed at Universal Studios from 11 AM to about 8 PM.

I did the following activities at Universal Studios in order:

  • The World-Famous Studio Tour
  • Lunch (ate food from Vietnamese bakery)
  • Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey™
  • Jurassic World – The Ride
  • Revenge of the Mummy – The Ride
  • Panda Express for meal between lunch and dinner
  • TRANSFORMERS™: The Ride-3D
  • WaterWorld
  • Despicable Me Minion Mayhem

Advice for the Universal Studios Attractions Above

  • The World-Famous Studio Tour: Minor Profanity, Not Completely Child-Friendly
  • Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey™: Upside-Down Ride and You Can't Tell Where You'll Go
  • Jurassic World: You May Get Wet at the Big Drop
  • Revenge of the Mummy: Put Your Items in the Item Pouch Before Putting Down Your Lap Bar
  • TRANSFORMERS™: Toddlers Could Ride This
  • WaterWorld: Not For People Sensitive to Loud Noises or People Who Don't Like Getting Wet
  • Despicable Me Minion Mayhem: The Routine Body Scanner in the Introduction is a Joke
