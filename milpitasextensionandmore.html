Milpitas Extension

I went to Milpitas Extension from June 27 to July 21. My classes were from Mondays to Thursdays and from 10 AM to 2 PM. The only Monday we didn't attend these classes was July 4. There were 39 students and about eleven adults who were professors or assistants. Two students dropped out. We were divided into two groups.

Time Our Group Other Group
10 AM to 11 AM Mental Health and College Study Hall
11 AM to 12 PM HTML
12 PM to 1 PM Lunch
1 PM to 2 PM Study Hall Mental Health and College
On the first week, the adults gave us Subway for lunch. After the first week, they gave us Subway every Monday and Wednesday and Pizza Box every Tuesday and Thursday. Some guest speakers working at big companies would come to talk to us during lunch. On the last day, July 21, we 37 students had a final exam in our mental health class and each got certificates on July 21. We also had to present our websites in our study hall from July 19 to July 21.
Before you ask, yes, I presented this website to my class because this website was made for Milpitas Extension.

Family Reunion

From late July 2022 to August 7, 2022, I had a family reunion. I saw fourteen of my second cousins and all seven of my first cousins (at the time) on my dad's side. My paternal grandfather has six younger siblings and three of them attended the reunion. Ten of my second cousins are the grandchildren of my great-uncle and four of my second cousins are the grandchildren of my other great-uncle. My great-aunt and her husband stayed in my house from late July 2022 to August 9, 2022. Sadly, my aunt, my dad's youngest sister, didn't come because she was pregnant with her first daughter.

End of Summer

On August 8, I got my braces off after having them for two years. Seven days later, I got retainers.
I started tenth grade on August 10. I didn't really like that year of school because I had a class that I kept blaming for keeping me up late at night.
